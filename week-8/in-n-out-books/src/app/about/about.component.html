Cesar Millan

Cesar was born in 1969, to a small rural town of Culiacán, Mexico. He learned about dogs through his grandfather. And saw on TV and movies how other people interacted with dogs, and realized what they were doing wrong. He dreamed of becoming the world’s best dog trainer. In 1990, he decided to move to America to pursue his dream. By 2002, he was featured in the Los Angeles Times and earned a reputation with his calm-assertive approach and being able to walk several dogs at a time.
